What is Solving Zero about?

We are a small dedicated team that works around the clock to find the most cost effective ways that green energy can save you money and save the planet.

Our first goal is to provide well written research that explores just exactly how each piece of green technology works and how can it save you money. By exploring our research and data you can make better informed decisions about how you can save money and the planet.

Currently at Solving Zero you can find information on the greenest and most affordable energy retailers. In the future we hope to expand our suggestions to buying and installing solar, buying EVs, remodelling and building homes, and everything else to help you reach your personal net zero carbon emissions.
